POLICY-ACCRUAL-01 — Synthetic close policy
Version: 2026.1
Entity: Nordix Services AB

Supplier invoices must be checked for duplicate identity and posting cutoff.
Services extending beyond the reporting period require a documented allocation.
The current period recognizes service received through the period end.
The unconsumed portion is proposed as a prepaid asset for human review.
Automation and model output are advisory and cannot approve, post, or lock.
An authorized human reviewer must record a rationale against the evidence snapshot.
